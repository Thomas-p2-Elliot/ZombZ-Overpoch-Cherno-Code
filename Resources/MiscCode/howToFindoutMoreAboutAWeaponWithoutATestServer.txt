HOW TO FIND OUT WHAT A WEAPON IS LIKE IN OVERPOCH WITHOUT USING A TEST SERVER


1. Open Overpoch, escape out to menu, press ALT+E, load Utes(faster)
2. Add Center, then group
3. Add unit, before press ok put this info into the 'Init Expression' box:

player addWeapon "WEAPON_NAME"; player addMagazine "MAGAZINE_NAME";

Get weapon and magazine names from this url: http://pastebin.com/raw.php?i=mYpAJETh

4. Press Preview, Drop gear until you find ur gun...
